    Patagonia Volunteer Vacation

    (39)
    4.7 out of 5 stars. 39 reviews

    Days: 13

    Activity Level: 3

    Member: from $3,985

    Activity Level and Qualifications

    This trip is rated Moderate [3]. Trip members may be novices to trail maintenance or restoration but they must be in good physical condition. Volunteers should be comfortable hiking up to six miles most days on rolling or mountainous terrain with some steep ascents and uneven trails. The work sites will vary but generally will be a 2 to 3 mile walk each way (up to 6 miles total), each day and then working for a full day on trail building or maintenance.

    To maximize your enjoyment of the trip, to build up stamina and to avoid sore muscles, we suggest beginning a regular exercise regimen at least three months prior to departure.

    This trip is rated Moderate [3], in a large part due to visiting areas located at 8,000-11,000' in elevation. Trip members may be novice volunteers in good physical condition.
